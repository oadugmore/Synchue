Sync,hue

Color coordinated challenges

Sync,hue is a physics-based platforming game where everything is controlled by color. The player, the environment, and even enemies have synchronized movement with objects of the corresponding color. However, this doesn't mean you have direct control over everything — you simply have a blue button and an orange button. You must discover how these and other colors affect the world around you to pass each challenge.

A project by Owen Dugmore.
